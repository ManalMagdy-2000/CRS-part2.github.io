About Us

Crisis Relief Services (CRS) is an NGO (Non-Government Organization) that aims to help people who are facing crises arising from natural disasters such as flood and earthquakes , our mission is to -engage the local population and corporate organizations in response to disasters. -Train local volunteers and partners in disaster management and -Promote disaster risk reduction among vulnerable communities.
